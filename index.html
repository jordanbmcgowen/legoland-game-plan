Insider Knowledge

Pro Tips

Tricks and tips to make your day even smoother.

⏱
5-min Fastrack cooldown between uses — plan your pairs wisely (Emmet's + Unikitty, Dragon + NINJAGO, Coast Cruise + Coastersaurus)
📱
LEGOLAND app shows live wait times — check before walking to a ride. It also does Fastrack scanning.
🚀
LEGO Galaxy land is brand new (March 2026) and will be the most crowded area — hit it at rope drop before anyone else.
🧱
Minifigure trading: bring small LEGO minifigures from home and trade with park employees wearing lanyards. Jackson will love it!
👶
Stroller rental: $17/day at The Beginning — worth it for a 4-year-old who will walk 3+ miles.
💧
Free water refill stations throughout the park — bring reusable bottles and save money.
👨‍👩‍👦
Parent Swap: if one parent wants to ride something Jackson can't, use parent rider swap so nobody waits in line twice.
🛝
Need a break? NINJAGO Training Camp, Hideaways playground, and Benny's Playship are great no-line play areas when Jackson needs to decompress.
🍎
Granny's Apple Fries near the front of the park are the #1 must-try LEGOLAND snack. Don't leave without trying them!
🚻
Restroom locations: The Beginning, Fun Town, Castle Hill, NINJAGO World, and Dino Valley. Plan stops accordingly.
